Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services

Who is eligible for tax refunds?
Most employees who have paid taxes in the last 6 years may be eligible for refunds. This includes full-time, part-time, and freelance workers in certain circumstances.
How long does the initial check take?
Our initial assessment typically takes 24-48 hours after we receive your completed survey and necessary documents.
Is my privacy guaranteed?
Yes, we take your privacy seriously. All your information is encrypted and protected. We never share your data with third parties without your explicit consent.
What are the service fees?
We work on a success-based fee model. If we don't recover money for you, you don't pay anything. Our fee is a percentage of the amount we successfully recover for you.
Can I communicate in Arabic or Hebrew?
Absolutely! Our team is fluent in both Arabic and Hebrew, and we can provide all communications in your preferred language.
